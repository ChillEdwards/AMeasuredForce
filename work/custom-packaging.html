Packaging Design

A Measured Force partners with brands and packaging vendors to develop solutions that both protect and promote a product. Every detail — from shape and construction to interior layout and exterior presentation — is designed to create an unforgettable unboxing experience.

M1NT Card Cases

For M1NT, AMF designed a premium card case featuring metallic color-shift finishes with chrome bezel detailing. The project included 3D product renderings, structural design with explosion views, and multiple colorway development — from metallic silver and pale gold to metallic purple and caviar black.

Material selection defines the tactile experience and overall impression — from the weight of a lid to the texture of a finish.

RMC Sample Boxes

For Representative Materials Company, AMF designed and produced a series of custom sample boxes used by the sales team across North America and internationally. Each box was engineered to present stainless steel product samples in a polished, organized format with branded exteriors and precision-cut interior layouts.

Cross-Category

AMF's packaging work extends across consumer goods, luxury accessories, and specialty retail. Each project follows the same rigorous process: understanding the brand, engineering the structure, designing the artwork, selecting the materials, and delivering a finished product that elevates the unboxing experience.
