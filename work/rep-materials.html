Brand & Digital Infrastructure

A Measured Force partnered with Representative Materials Company from its founding to build the brand, marketing systems, and digital platforms that would carry a small startup into a multimillion-dollar manufacturing operation with global reach.

Brand Foundation

From the ground up, AMF built RMC's brand identity and creative foundation — defining its visual language, product marketing, and digital strategy. What began in a friend's home office now operates from large warehouse facilities with a nationwide sales network and manufacturing partnerships across North America, India, Thailand, China, and Saudi Arabia.

Through creative leadership and disciplined execution, we built a brand that stands shoulder-to-shoulder with decades-old manufacturing giants.

Digital Ecosystem

AMF directed the design and development of multiple websites and digital tools, including a modernized product library, interactive spec sheets, and a dedicated portal where external sales representatives can create accounts, access marketing materials, and order trade show supplies.

Global Coordination

Beyond creative direction, AMF manages global teams across Serbia, Ukraine, Belgium, and Los Angeles — coordinating initiatives that align marketing, sales, and manufacturing goals. From content creation and graphic design to industrial design for marketing materials and trade show environments, every touchpoint is considered.
