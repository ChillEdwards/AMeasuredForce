Brand Transformation

A Measured Force led Zuma Lighting's creative evolution — modernizing an established brand while simultaneously building Zuma Bollards from the ground up. The result is a cohesive brand ecosystem that supports product discovery, sales enablement, and marketing growth across both companies.

Zuma Lighting

AMF spearheaded a complete brand transformation and digital overhaul. The redesigned website functions as a powerful sales tool for reps and distributors, featuring a modernized product library, interactive configurators for creating custom SKUs, project showcases, and clear communication of Zuma's custom manufacturing capabilities.

The evolution of Zuma Lighting and the creation of Zuma Bollards reflect a singular approach — blending strategy, design, and execution to transform ideas into market-leading platforms.

Zuma Bollards

AMF created Zuma Bollards from scratch — shaping its entire identity, positioning, and go-to-market strategy. From naming and branding to product marketing, web design, and content strategy, we built a new brand presence that now stands as a trusted name in architectural security products.

Design Process

Every detail was guided by visual direction, from high-fidelity mockups and homepage concepts to mobile-first design systems. AMF also leads cross-functional initiatives aligning marketing with sales and manufacturing goals — overseeing campaigns, product launches, and digital assets that support growth across North America.
