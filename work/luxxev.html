E-Commerce & Brand Launch

LuxxEV had a logo and a vision for premium air fresheners designed for Tesla interiors. A Measured Force took that foundation and built everything else — brand identity, e-commerce platform, content library, packaging, and a go-to-market strategy that established LuxxEV as a modern lifestyle brand.

Brand Identity

AMF established a clear visual identity that reflects the minimalist, high-end aesthetic of the Tesla ecosystem. We developed the color palette, typography system, and brand guidelines that set the tone for every touchpoint — digital and physical.

While the products were already designed, we created the identity, infrastructure, and storytelling that turned those designs into a brand built to last.

E-Commerce Platform

We designed and launched a custom e-commerce website that presents the product through a refined, intuitive experience. The platform was built to convert — with product storytelling, scent profiles, and a seamless checkout flow that matches the premium positioning of the brand.

Content Production

Over four intensive days of content creation, AMF produced a library of evergreen assets for the website and for use across social media and marketing campaigns. Copywriting, photography, and videography worked together to tell a consistent brand story and highlight the details that make LuxxEV unique.
