Summary

A Measured Force partnered with THERAsurf, a nonprofit dedicated to helping individuals with disabilities experience the healing power of surfing, to modernize the organization's brand and digital presence. Our work focused on redesigning the website, refining the visual identity, and strengthening social media communication to better showcase THERAsurf's mission, programs, and community impact.

Challenge

THERAsurf had built a strong reputation through its programs and community involvement, but its brand and digital presence did not fully reflect the scale of its work. The organization needed a modern website capable of clearly communicating its mission, highlighting its programs, and supporting donor engagement. The existing platform lacked the structure, design, and functionality required to effectively reach new supporters, attract corporate sponsors, and drive fundraising.

Solution

AMF redesigned THERAsurf's website from the ground up, creating a modern digital platform that clearly communicates the organization's mission, showcases its programs, and highlights the real impact of the organization's work, and provides clear pathways for supporters to get involved through volunteering, partnerships, and donations.

Alongside the website launch, AMF helped modernize the organization's brand presence and developed a more cohesive social media approach to improve communication with supporters and strengthen community engagement.

Key Results

Modern nonprofit website platform
Refined brand and visual identity
Improved social media presence
Clearer communication of mission
Increased donations from individuals and businesses
Acquired grant fundraising
