Modernizing a nonprofit's digital presence to better communicate its mission, drive donations, and streamline community engagement.

Nonprofit & UX Design

THERAsurf is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to giving children with special needs and those from underprivileged communities the chance to experience the healing power of the ocean through surf therapy. They approached AMF to refresh their website with two clear goals: inspire families to join events and create a compelling platform to attract donors.

UX Research

AMF began with user research and protopersona development to understand the motivations and pain points of THERAsurf's core audiences — families, donors, and volunteers. This research directly informed the information architecture, donation flow, and registration system that would form the backbone of the redesigned platform.

The project focused on improving the donation flow — making it simpler, more engaging, and more rewarding for supporters.

Design & Development

AMF redesigned the website from the ground up, creating a modern platform that clearly communicates the mission, showcases programs, highlights real impact, and provides clear pathways for involvement through volunteering, partnerships, and donations. Both front-end and back-end were built to streamline participation and help THERAsurf stay organized.

Ongoing Partnership

Following this project, AMF's founder joined THERAsurf's board of directors as Director of Marketing — now overseeing all marketing, social media, website maintenance, grant applications, and volunteer management. Together, the team has raised significant funding to continue providing surf therapy to children in Los Angeles and internationally.
